Find Photos

Instantly source clean, white-background product images for branded goods

Graphic Design Shopify Listing User Onboarding

Find Photos is a Shopify app for resellers and brick-and-mortar stores selling branded products. Instead of taking your own photos, just select a product and the app finds clean, white-background images for you—no uploads, editing, or design skills needed. It works best for well-known brands like liquor, cosmetics, and shoes.

For the App Store listing, I needed to communicate a simple value proposition: skip the photography, get professional images instantly. The challenge was showing both how easy the flow is and why it matters—better product photos mean more clicks, more trust, and faster time to launch. The images walk through the workflow step by step, building confidence before the download.
