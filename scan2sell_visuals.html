Scan2Sell

Barcode-based product upload for fast inventory digitization

Graphic Design Shopify Listing User Onboarding

Scan2Sell is a Shopify app for merchants managing large physical inventories. Instead of manual data entry, users scan product barcodes to instantly auto-fill professional images, titles, and descriptions—getting products online in seconds.

Before designing the App Store listing, I needed to fully understand the product: how the four upload methods work (camera scanner, Bluetooth gun, photo capture, CSV import), how the AI auto-fill behaves, and how it integrates with Shopify POS. The challenge was distilling a multi-step technical workflow into static images that follow Shopify's strict listing guidelines while answering the user's core questions—how do I start, what are my options, and what happens after I scan. It's similar to creating an easy-to-understand onboarding flow, but entirely through static visuals.
